1 Then Bildad the Shuhite replied:
 2 “Dominion and awe belong to God; he establishes order in the heights of heaven.
 3 Can his forces be numbered? On whom does his light not rise? 4 How then can a mortal be righteous before God? How can one born of woman be pure? 5 If even the moon is not bright and the stars are not pure in his eyes, 6 how much less a mortal, who is but a maggot— a human being, who is only a worm!”
